Shipping, Order Tracking & Delivery

A: We offer free shipping on all orders above $50. Orders are usually delivered within 5-7 business days.

Q: How can I track my order?

A: You can track your order using the tracking number sent to your email or by logging into your account.

Q: What should I do if my order is delayed?

A: If your order is delayed, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Return, Replacement & Exchange

Q: How can I return an item?

A: To return an item, please contact our customer service within 30 days of receiving your order.

Q: Can I exchange an item?

A: Yes, you can exchange items within 30 days. Please follow the instructions provided in our return policy.

Q: What is the process for getting a replacement?

A: If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact us to arrange a replacement.

Cancellations & Modifications

Q: Can I cancel my order?

A: Orders can be canceled within 1 hour of placing them. After that, we may not be able to process cancellations.

Q: How can I modify my order?

A: You can modify your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support team.

Q: What if my order has already been shipped?

A: Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be modified or canceled. You can return it after receiving it.

Payments & Refunds

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods.

Q: How can I get a refund?

A: Refunds are processed to the original payment method. Please contact customer service for more details.

Q: My payment failed. What should I do?

A: If your payment fails, please check your payment details and try again. Contact support if the issue persists.

Warranty

Q: What does the warranty cover?

A: Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and workmanship issues. It does not cover damage from misuse.

Q: How long is the warranty period?

A: The warranty period is 1 year from the date of purchase. Please check your warranty card for details.

Q: How can I claim a warranty?

A: To claim a warranty, please contact customer service with your proof of purchase and details of the issue.
